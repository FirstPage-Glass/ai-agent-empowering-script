# Part 6: Frequently Asked Questions ❓

### Q: I don't know how to code at all. Can I still use this? 🤔
**A: Yes.** OpenCode Desktop has a graphical interface — use it like any other app. All you need to be able to do is type.

---

### Q: Does it cost money? 💰
**A: No.** OpenCode Desktop comes with free models built-in, ready to use right after installation.

If you want more powerful models later, here are your options:

| Plan | Cost | Details |
|---|---|---|
| 🆓 **Free Models** | **Free** ✅ | Built-in, ready to use immediately |
| 🟢 **OpenCode Go** | $5 first month, then $10/month | Low-cost subscription, includes many powerful open-source models with generous limits |
| 🔵 **OpenCode Zen** | Pay-as-you-go | Pay only for what you use, includes Claude, GPT-5, and other top-tier models |
| 🔑 **Bring Your Own API Key** | Depends on provider | Use your own OpenAI / Anthropic API key |

> 💡 **Recommendation:** Try the free models first. If you find them useful, consider upgrading. OpenCode Go is only $5 for the first month — a great way to try more powerful models: [opencode.ai/go](https://opencode.ai/go)

---

### Q: Is my data safe? 🔒
**A: It depends on the model you use.** OpenCode itself does not store your data. Free models may collect data for improvement purposes. We recommend not uploading company confidential information and following your company's data security policies.

---

### Q: Does AI make mistakes? ⚠️
**A: Yes.** AI is not perfect. It may:
- 🤷 Misunderstand your intent
- ❌ Give incorrect information
- 🤪 Produce nonsensical content

**So always double-check important information yourself.**

---

### Q: How is OpenCode different from ChatGPT? 🤷
**A: ChatGPT tells you what to do. OpenCode does it for you.** OpenCode can directly modify files, organize data, and execute actions — not just give you suggestions.

---

### Q: Where can I go if I have questions? 🆘
**A:**
- 📖 OpenCode official docs: [opencode.ai/docs](https://opencode.ai/docs)
- 💻 OpenCode GitHub: [github.com/anomalyco/opencode](https://github.com/anomalyco/opencode)
- 👥 Ask colleagues who are already using it

---

> 💬 **Final reminder: AI is a tool, not a magic wand. Think of it as a very smart assistant — but the final judgment is still yours.**
