# Part 4: Daily Scenarios 🚀

> 💡 Each scenario has a **sample file** in the `examples/` folder. Download them and practice!

---

## Scenario 1: Writing Emails ✉️

**You say:**
```
Write an email to client Ms. Wang that includes:
1. Thank her for attending our product demo last week
2. Attach the product catalog PDF
3. Ask if she'd like to schedule a one-on-one demo
4. Use a professional but friendly tone
```

**OpenCode will:**
- ✅ Write the complete email for your review
- ✅ You can ask it to adjust the tone, add or remove content
- ✅ When satisfied, copy and paste into Outlook / Gmail

> 📂 **Try this example:** [`examples/email-context.md`](examples/email-context.md) — contains client background and meeting details, open it with OpenCode and practice.
